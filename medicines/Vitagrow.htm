Vitagrow (Vitamin A,D,B1, B2, B6, C, E, Nicotinamide and Cod liver oil)

Therapeutic Group: Vitamins and Minerals

Presentation

Vitagrow : Each 5ml contains - Vitamin A 2000 IU Vitamin D 200 IU Vitamin B1 0.70 mg Vitamin B2 0.85 mg Vitamin B6 0.35 mg Vitamin C 17.50 mg Vitamin E 1.50 IU Nicotinamide 9.00 mg Cod Liver Oil 0.10 gm

Descriptions

Vitagrow is a special preparation of Vitamin A, D, B1, B2, B6, C, E, Nicotinamide and cod liver oil. it is a multivitamin syrup with orange flavor has good taste, and easily taken.

Indications

To increase multivitamin to body for good health. Prevention of vitamin lacking symptom for growing child, lack of appetite child, including patient under rehabilitating period.

This product is contraindicated in patients with a known hypersensitivity to any of the ingredients.

Overdose

In case of overdosage, initially epigastric pain, diarrhoea and vomiting can occur. Should seek emergency medical attention in case of overdose.
