Presentation

Each ml contains Polyethylene Glycol 400 BP 4 mg and Propylene Glycol BP 3 mg.

Descriptions

Filtear contains Polyethylene Glycol 400 (0. 4%) and Propylene Glycol (0. 3%). Polyethylene Glycol 400 (0. 4%) and Propylene Glycol (0. 3%) are eye lubricants. Eye lubricants keep the eye moist, help to protect the eye from injury and infection, and decrease symptoms of dry eye such as burning, irritation and feeling as if something is in the eye. It is scientifically formulated to shield eyes from dry eye discomfort so that eyes feel moist and refreshed longer.

Indications

It is indicated for the temporary relief of burning and irritation due to dry eye.

Dosage and Administration

For Adults and Children: Instill 1 or 2 drops in the affected eye(s) as needed.

Side Effects

Generally well tolerated. A very serious allergic reaction to this drug is rare. It should not be used if allergic condition occurs to any ingredients of the product.

Precautions

Do not touch tip of container to any surface to avoid contamination.

Contraindications

It is contraindicated in patients with known sensitivity to any ingredient in this formulation. Storage Protect from light. Store in cool and dry place. Keep out of the reach of children. Do not use more than 4 weeks after opening.

Commercial Packaging

Each plastic dropper bottle containing 10 ml of Sterile Eye Drops.
